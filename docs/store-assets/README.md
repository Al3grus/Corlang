## Screenshots — what to capture

Take them on your phone (power + volume-down), then drop the PNGs into
`docs/store-assets/screenshots/`. Your build has real streak/progress data, which looks
much better in the listing than a fresh install's empty states.

Shoot in this order — the first two are what most people actually see in the store:

1. **Today dashboard** — with the GoalRing part-filled and a live streak.
2. **A lesson exercise mid-answer** — ideally a translate or multiple-choice step.
3. **Review / SRS** — a flashcard with cards due.
4. **The level journey** — the A0→B2 progress map.
5. **AI tutor chat** — a short exchange in Croatian (Learn tab).
6. *(optional)* Quiz results or a mock-exam screen.

Requirements Play enforces: PNG or JPEG, portrait 9:16-ish, each side between 320 and
3840 px. A native phone screenshot already satisfies all of this — **do not crop or resize**.

Avoid in-frame: the status bar clock reading something odd, notification badges, and any
personal content in the AI chat.

Once they're in the folder, ask Claude to check dimensions and draft the per-screenshot
caption text for the listing.
