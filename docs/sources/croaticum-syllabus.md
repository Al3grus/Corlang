## Course ladder (semester program)

Six sequential levels, each one semester:

| Croaticum level | CEFR |
|---|---|
| Jezične vježbe 1 | A1 → A2 |
| Jezične vježbe 2 | B1.1 |
| Jezične vježbe 3 | B1.2 |
| Jezične vježbe 4 | B2.1 |
| Jezične vježbe 5 | B2.2 |
| Jezične vježbe 6 | C1 |

Per-level focus (from the published course descriptions):
- **Level 1 (A1–A2):** fundamentals of phonology, morphology (noun declension, personal/
  possessive pronouns, descriptive adjectives, numbers, present tense of common verb groups,
  modal verbs, past tense, futur I, reflexive verbs) and basic syntax; basic conversation,
  reading/writing simple texts; everyday topics (family, professions, weather…).
- **Level 2 (B1.1):** use in different situations; education, work, health, tourism topics;
  genitive with numbers; imperative.
- **Level 3 (B1.2):** texts of suitable difficulty on contemporary/cultural topics;
  interrogative and relative pronouns; passive participle.
- **Level 4 (B2.1):** reading, analysing, reproducing texts (technology, heritage);
  diminutives/augmentatives; direct and indirect speech.
- **Level 5 (B2.2):** sophisticated materials (environment, global topics); verb valency,
  collocations, concord, connectors.
- **Level 6 (C1):** tenses, aspect and verbs in context; modern literary and professional
  texts; style and advanced syntax.

## Official tests

Croaticum tests Croatian for various purposes (incl. citizenship/residence certificates) at
CEFR levels, at FFZG Zagreb (and Macquarie University, Sydney). The test has five components —
listening comprehension, reading comprehension, grammatical structures, writing, speaking —
matching NN 100/2021 (see `nn-exam-regulations.md`). Sample tasks: see
`croaticum-b1-sample.md`. Application ≥10 days ahead with ID.

- *Dobro došli 1–2* (Barešić) — referenced in the ASOO curriculum bibliography

## Free e-courses (ffzg-ecourse)

- **a1.ffzg.unizg.hr** — E-tečaj A1.hr: 80 units × ~45 min (listening text or words,
  pronunciation, repetition, writing, comprehension), EN/ES interfaces, free (Google/Facebook
  login). Made by FFZG with the Central State Office for Croats Abroad.
- **a2.ffzg.unizg.hr** — E-tečaj A2.hr: 80 units, same format, 8 modules of 10 lessons.

These pace the app's daily plan Phases 1–2.
