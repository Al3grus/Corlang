### Skill outcomes (summary of verbatim descriptors)
Listening: understands very slow, clearly articulated speech about self/immediate surroundings;
numbers, prices, clock time; simple directions. Reading: personal data from newspapers, posters,
schedules; forms (visa, hotel); public signs; short postcards/messages. Spoken interaction:
introductions, greetings; simple Q&A about immediate needs; simple purchases; numbers/quantities
/prices/time; asking about where people live, who they know, what they own. Spoken production:
personal information, describing where one lives. Writing: forms with personal data; greeting
card; simple postcard; short note about location/meeting; simple sentences about self.

### Topics (hours)
Zemlje i jezici (14) · Kad će taj vikend? (12) · Kvaliteta života (12) · Planovi, želje,
mogućnosti (12) · Transport i komunikacija (12) · Odnosi, izgled, budućnost (12) · Tko radi,

### Skill outcomes (summary)
Listening: simple everyday conversation addressed slowly and directly; main topic of slow, clear
discussion; essential info from short recorded messages; TV news gist with visuals. Reading:
simple news items with numbers/names; simple personal letters; prospectuses; classified ads;
device instructions; simple stories. Spoken interaction: transactions in shops/post/bank; public
transport and tickets; ordering food/drink; likes/dislikes; arranging meetings; apologizing.
Spoken production: describing self/family/people, home, education, jobs, hobbies, past events.
Writing: short notes/messages; simple event reports (what/when/where); everyday-life
descriptions; CV-style form data; short letters with greetings/thanks; linking with i/ali/jer
and najprije/zatim/potom/kasnije.

### Skill outcomes (summary)
Listening: clearly articulated everyday conversation; main ideas of longer discussions in
standard language; radio news gist; simple technical instructions. Reading: short news articles
on current topics; columns/interviews with stances; guessing unknown words from context;
standard letters; story plots. Spoken interaction: open/maintain/close conversation on familiar
topics; travel-agency and travel situations; expressing and responding to feelings; opinions in
informal discussion; polite (dis)agreement. Spoken production: telling a story; detailed
experience descriptions with feelings; dreams/hopes/ambitions; explaining plans; retelling a
book/film with reactions. Writing: connected texts on familiar topics with opinions; experience
/event texts; personal letters with news and retellings; responding to ads; short CV.

## B2 / C1 / C2 (continuation levels — summarized)

- **B2 (140 h):** noun-class particularities (a-, e-, i-vrsta), predicate nominative vs
  instrumental, agreement (sročnost) incl. tata/varalica/braća types, numeric nouns, se-passive,
  verbal nouns + complements, iterative conditional, word formation (agent nouns, diminutives,
  augmentatives), futur II vs perfective present, coordinate clause types with clitic placement
  after conjunctions, negation incl. Slavic genitive, direct/indirect speech with clitic order.
- **C1 (280 h):** words-in-context series, affirmative stance, reproach, similarity/difference,
  summarizing, speech preparation; grammar approached correctively; heavy idiom/collocation work.
- **C2 (124 h):** all grammar assumed; corrective only; topics set by learners; nuance,
  polysemy, collocations, idioms, colloquialisms; native-speaker teacher recommended.
