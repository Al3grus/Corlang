# Official exam regulations (Narodne novine)

## NN 100/2021 — the B1 language exam (long-term residence)

- **Source:** *Pravilnik o polaganju ispita iz poznavanja hrvatskoga jezika i latiničnog pisma
  u postupku odobrenja dugotrajnog boravišta* (NN 100/2021).
- **URL:** https://narodne-novine.nn.hr/clanci/sluzbeni/2021_09_100_1800.html
- **Fetched:** 2026-07-09
- **This is the regulation that defines the actual 5-section exam** conducted by authorized
  institutions (Croaticum, CEHAS, Rijeka School of Croatian Studies, licensed adult-education
  institutions). Croaticum's own sample tasks (see `croaticum-b1-sample.md`) follow this format.

**Level:** B1 ("razini B1").

**Structure (Article 4(3)) — five components:**
1. Razumijevanje slušanoga teksta — listening comprehension
2. Razumijevanje čitanoga teksta — reading comprehension
3. Poznavanje jezičnih struktura — knowledge of language (grammar) structures
4. Pisano sporazumijevanje — written communication
5. Usmeno sporazumijevanje — oral communication

Components 1–4 are written; component 5 is oral (Article 4(4)).

**Pass rule:** minimum **60% of points** ("najmanje 60% bodova") on each of listening, reading
and grammar structures; written and oral communication are graded **zadovoljio / nije
zadovoljio** (pass/fail). The exam is passed only by passing **every** section (Article 5(1)).

**Certificate (Article 6(3)):** institution name, certificate number, place/date, candidate
identity, exam date, pass status, achieved percentages, purpose, signature and seal.

→ App usage: the mock-exam runner reproduces the 5 sections and applies exactly this pass rule
(≥60% × 3 scored sections + 2 × pass).

## NN 6/2021 — citizenship procedure

- **Source:** *Pravilnik o načinu provjere poznavanja hrvatskog jezika i latiničnog pisma,
  hrvatske kulture i društvenog uređenja u postupcima stjecanja hrvatskog državljanstva*
  (NN 6/2021, in force 22 Jan 2021).
- **URL:** https://narodne-novine.nn.hr/clanci/sluzbeni/2021_01_6_124.html
- **Fetched:** 2026-07-09

**Language requirement (Article 2):** proof of **B1** Croatian ("B1 stupnja znanja hrvatskog
jezika") via a certificate from an authorized institution (i.e., the NN 100/2021-style exam or
an equivalent institutional certificate), or completed schooling in Croatia. Exemptions exist
(Article 3: long-term refugees, certain returnees).

**Culture & society questionnaire (Article 6):** separate from the language exam — 15 questions
drawn from a published bank of 110 (Annex 1), 60 minutes, pass = ≥10 correct, administered at
MUP (police administration) or a diplomatic mission.

→ App usage: the B1 exam milestone in `levels.json` cites both regulations: NN 100/2021 for the
exam format, NN 6/2021 for the citizenship pathway that accepts the B1 certificate.
